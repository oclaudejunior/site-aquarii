05 — Motion

Motion & interaction

Motion is restrained. Three behaviors carry the brand: icon rotation on hover (45°→90°, 300ms), menu weight transition (100ms), and flutuar — a slow vertical float on the scroll-down indicator.

06 — Icons

Icon system

Icons are inline SVG, stroke-based, with consistent stroke-linecap="round" and stroke-linejoin="round". Color inherits from stroke attribute (white on dark, black on light). Sizes follow three discrete steps.
